Cat

In ancient times cats were worshipped as gods; they haven't forgotten this.
- Terry Pratchett

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Origin: Domenstication occurred in Western Asia around 7500 BC
  • Etymology: The Latin word "cattus" is derived from Coptic "šau", meaning "tomcat"
  • Size: Averages about 46 cm in length and 23–25 cm in height

The cat is a domestic species of small carnivorous mammal. It is the only domesticated species in the family Felidae.

Quokka

I travel way too much to have any pets. But if I could have one, I'd want a quokka. They're basically small kangaroos native to Western Australia.
- Justine Larbalestier

  • Scientific Name: Setonix brachyurus
  • Origin: Found on some smaller islands off the coast of Western Australia
  • Etymology: The word "quokka" is derived from a Nyungar word, which was probably "gwaga"
  • Size: Averages about 50 cm in length

The quokka, also known as the short-tailed scrub wallaby, is a small macropod about the size of a domestic cat. It is the only member of the genus Setonix.
